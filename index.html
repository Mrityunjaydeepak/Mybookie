About Mybookie

Mybookie is built for readers who want less noise — and more meaning.

Mybookie — The e-Library is an upcoming ebook solution designed around one simple idea: discovery should be effortless, reading should feel calm, and your library should stay organized. We’re building a modern platform where you can explore curated ebooks, save what matters, and keep your reading journey consistent — without getting lost in clutter.

Fast search Curated collections Clean dark UI Reading momentum

1) Discovery that respects your time

Mybookie highlights what’s worth reading. Think: topic filters, intent-based browsing, and curated shelves that cut through the noise.

2) A library you can actually manage

Save books, build lists, and keep track of what you want to read next. No more “I’ll remember it later.” Your library stays tidy.

3) A dark experience that feels premium

A calm, dark gray theme with subtle motion. Smooth transitions, clean typography, and UI that stays out of the way while you focus.

4) Built to scale into a complete solution

Today: a polished library experience. Next: accounts, syncing, reading progress, recommendations, and more. Mybookie is designed to grow.

Roadmap (high-level)

A simple rollout plan to turn Mybookie into a full ebook solution.

1
Launch the core library experience Fast search, categories, curated shelves, preview, and clean UI foundations.
2
Accounts + saved library sync Saved books across devices, lists, and a personal dashboard for reading intent.
3
Reading progress + smart recommendations Continue reading, lightweight tracking, and suggestions based on your taste — not hype.
