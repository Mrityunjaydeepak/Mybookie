FAQ

Quick answers for the Mybookie concept.

Is Mybookie only for gamers?

No. It’s for anyone who wants reading to feel effortless. Gaming elements are optional—but motivating.

What do I gain from typing challenges?

Speed, accuracy, focus, and a measurable skill curve—plus XP and progression to keep you consistent.

Can I just read without playing?

Yes. “Read Mode” is calm. “Play Mode” is competitive. Switch anytime.
